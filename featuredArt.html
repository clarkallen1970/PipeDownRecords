THIS MONTH'S FEATURED ARTIST IS:

MANDY STUART-

Born and raised in central Kansas, this young, up and coming singer/songwriter grew up listening to her parent's records. She credits this as a large influence on the way she sings and wrties her music today.

"Growing up, I was blessed to be exposed to the greats, like Carly Simon, Janis Joplin, and Linda Ronstadt," says Stuart.

She counts her blessings for being raised in an environment where she was exposed to such great music. Her accoustic style is unique, however, you can't help but hear the influence of artists of the past.

Pipe Down Records is proud to count her as on of the many new artists who have recorded their works at our studio.

Her latest CD is called ENCHANTED.

The Millard Music shop is offering a $2.00 Internet Coupon that can be used at their store on this or any other CD from our FEATURED ARTIST OF THE MONTH.
